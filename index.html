Our Advanced Medical Equipment

Cutting-edge technology for accurate diagnostics and effective treatments.

World-Class Facilities

At AIUTH, we invest in the latest medical technology to ensure our patients receive the highest standard of care. Our equipment is regularly updated and maintained by our team of biomedical engineers.

From diagnostic imaging to surgical robotics, our facilities rival the best hospitals in the region. We combine advanced technology with our expert medical staff to achieve exceptional outcomes.

3T Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)

High-resolution imaging for detailed visualization of soft tissues with reduced scan times.

Used for: Neurology, Orthopedics, Oncology

128-Slice CT Scanner

Advanced computed tomography for high-speed, low-dose imaging with exceptional detail.

Used for: Trauma, Cardiology, Pulmonary

Robotic Surgical System

Precision surgical robotics for minimally invasive procedures with enhanced dexterity.

Used for: Urology, Gynecology, General Surgery

Linear Accelerator

Advanced radiation therapy delivering precisely targeted treatment to cancer cells.

Used for: Oncology, Radiation Therapy

4D Echocardiography

Real-time cardiac imaging with volumetric assessment for comprehensive heart evaluation.

Used for: Cardiology, Pediatric Cardiology

Hybrid Operating Room

Combining advanced imaging with surgical capabilities for complex procedures in one space.

Used for: Vascular Surgery, Neurosurgery, Cardiothoracic

Biomedical Engineering Department

Our dedicated team of biomedical engineers ensures all equipment is maintained to the highest standards and our staff is properly trained on its use.

We also collaborate with manufacturers and universities on research initiatives to develop new medical technologies.
